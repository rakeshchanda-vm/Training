Governance & Responsible AI
===========================

Andromeda was designed not just to accelerate AI adoption—but to do so responsibly, in alignment with the principles of transparency, accountability, and trustworthiness that define the insurance industry’s regulatory landscape.

As insurers face increased scrutiny around AI usage—from federal guidance (e.g., the NIST AI Risk Management Framework) to state-level implementation of the NAIC AI Model Bulletin—Andromeda embeds governance into every layer of the agentic system lifecycle. This ensures our clients and teams can build and operate intelligent systems with confidence, clarity, and control.

------------------------------------------------

   * - Vendor & Tool Risk Management
     - Supports plug-in model evaluation and vendor risk review workflows

Alignment to Insurance AI Regulation
------------------------------------

Andromeda’s governance model aligns with the guiding principles outlined in the NAIC AI Model Bulletin and NIST AI RMF, including:

* **Fair & Ethical AI Use:** Ensures agents do not make unchecked decisions on eligibility, pricing, or claims outcomes without oversight.
* **Accountability:** All agent activity is traceable to an initiating actor (human or system), with logs available for internal or regulatory audit.
* **Transparency:** Through structured prompt and response recording, stakeholders can understand how decisions were made—and why.
* **Security & Privacy:** Compliant with enterprise authentication, authorization, and data governance protocols across cloud and on-prem environments.

Built-In Guardrails and Audit Layers
------------------------------------

Every Andromeda-based solution benefits from a pre-integrated “trust layer” that includes:

* Structured logging and tagging of prompts, actions, and outputs
* Optional escalation rules for sensitive workflows (e.g., claims denial recommendations, underwriting decisions)
* Metadata capture for document sources, agent versions, and tools used
* Support for centralized review and approval workflows across delivery teams

Andromeda doesn’t just power AI—it governs it. With these foundations, ValueMomentum ensures that as clients accelerate innovation, they do so in a way that is auditable, explainable, and fit for the future
